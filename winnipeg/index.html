About

Hello — we’re Trident Studios.

For more than a decade we’ve been photographing weddings, families and quiet moments across Winnipeg and all of Manitoba.

Our approach is calm and observational. We believe the best photographs come from trust — from feeling at ease in front of the camera, from being seen instead of posed. We chase honest light, we wait for the small gestures, and we put as much craft into the final edit as we do into the moment itself.

Hundreds of weddings later, our goal hasn’t changed: to give you a record of your day so beautifully made you’ll hand it down. That’s it. That’s everything.

Good to Know

Winnipeg wedding FAQ.

Do you travel to Winnipeg for weddings?

Yes. We photograph weddings throughout Winnipeg and the surrounding area regularly. Travel from our Brandon studio to Winnipeg is included in our wedding collections.

Which Winnipeg venues do you photograph at?

We love photographing at The Fort Garry Hotel, Assiniboine Park and the Qualico Family Centre, The Gates on Roblin, Fort Gibraltar, Hawthorn Estates, Pinnacle Club, and around The Forks and the Exchange District.

Can we do our engagement session in downtown Winnipeg?

Of course. The Forks, the Exchange District, St. Boniface, and Assiniboine Park are all beautiful backdrops for a Winnipeg engagement session, especially at golden hour.

How far in advance should we book our Winnipeg wedding?

We take a limited number of weddings each year. For popular summer and autumn Saturdays in Winnipeg, we recommend booking 12 to 18 months in advance.
