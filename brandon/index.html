About

Hello — we’re Trident Studios.

For more than a decade we’ve been photographing weddings, families and quiet moments across Brandon, Westman, and the wide prairie country beyond.

Our approach is calm and observational. We believe the best photographs come from trust — from feeling at ease in front of the camera, from being seen instead of posed. We chase honest light, we wait for the small gestures, and we put as much craft into the final edit as we do into the moment itself.

Hundreds of weddings later, our goal hasn’t changed: to give you a record of your day so beautifully made you’ll hand it down. That’s it. That’s everything.

Good to Know

Brandon wedding FAQ.

Are you a Brandon-based wedding photographer?

Yes. Trident Studios is based right here in Brandon, Manitoba. We photograph weddings throughout the Wheat City and all of the Westman region, with no travel fee within Brandon.

Which Brandon-area venues do you photograph at?

We regularly photograph at the Riverbank Discovery Centre, the Keystone Centre, the Victoria Inn, the Dome Building downtown, and at Clear Lake and Elkhorn Resort in Riding Mountain National Park.

How far in advance should we book our Brandon wedding?

We take on a limited number of weddings each year. For peak summer and early-autumn dates in Brandon and Westman, most couples book 12 to 18 months ahead.

Do you photograph elopements around Riding Mountain and Clear Lake?

Absolutely. Intimate ceremonies at Clear Lake, Wasagaming, and across Riding Mountain National Park are some of our favourite days to document.
