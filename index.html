About

Hello — we’re Trident Studios.

For more than a decade we’ve been photographing weddings, families and quiet moments across Brandon, Winnipeg, and the wide prairie country in between.

Our approach is calm and observational. We believe the best photographs come from trust — from feeling at ease in front of the camera, from being seen instead of posed. We chase honest light, we wait for the small gestures, and we put as much craft into the final edit as we do into the moment itself.

Hundreds of weddings later, our goal hasn’t changed: to give you a record of your day so beautifully made you’ll hand it down. That’s it. That’s everything.

Good to Know

Frequently asked questions.

Where are you based, and where do you photograph weddings?

We’re based in Brandon, Manitoba, and we photograph weddings across Brandon, Winnipeg, Regina, and all of Manitoba and Saskatchewan — plus the occasional destination celebration.

Do you travel for weddings across the prairies?

Yes. We regularly travel from Brandon to Winnipeg, Regina, and points in between. Travel within the prairies is included in most of our wedding collections — just tell us your date and venue.

How far in advance should we book our wedding?

We take on a limited number of weddings each year. For peak summer and early-autumn Saturdays in Brandon, Winnipeg, and Regina, most couples book 12 to 18 months ahead.

Do you photograph engagements and elopements too?

Absolutely. Alongside full wedding days we photograph engagement sessions and intimate elopements throughout Manitoba and Saskatchewan, with the same calm, observational approach.

How do we check if our date is available?

Send us a note through the inquiry form below or email hello@tridentstudios.ca with your date and location, and we’ll be in touch within 48 hours.
